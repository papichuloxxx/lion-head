Our Story

A Hidden Gem Where
Mountain Meets Serenity

Nestled in the heart of Mashonaland Central, Lion's Head Mumurwi Resort is more than a destination — it's an experience that reconnects you with the raw beauty of Zimbabwe's wilderness.

Named after the majestic Lion's Head mountain — a striking rock formation that watches over the landscape — our resort sits beside a scenic dam surrounded by ancient rock art, rolling hills, and the timeless beauty of the African bushveld.

Whether you're seeking a peaceful weekend escape, an adventure-filled family outing, or a unique corporate retreat, Lion's Head offers the perfect blend of nature, culture, and comfort.
